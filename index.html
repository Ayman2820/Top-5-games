TOP 5 GAMES TO PLAY IN 2024

In the video game industry, AAA (Triple-A) is a buzzword used to classify video games produced or distributed by a mid-sized or major publisher, which typically have higher development and marketing budgets than other tiers of games. Here are some games you need to play before 2024 ends.

Must-Play Games of 2024

  1. Assassin's Creed Mirage

    An action-adventure stealth game intended to be reminiscent of older Assassin's Creed titles, being more linear and story-focused with fewer role-playing elements.

  2. Forza Horizon 5

    A 2021 racing video game set in a fictionalized version of Mexico, known for its stunning visuals and expansive open world.

  3. Ghost of Tsushima

    An action-adventure game where you control a samurai on a quest to protect Tsushima Island during the first Mongol invasion of Japan.

  4. Marvel's Spider-Man 2

    Experience the dual adventures of Peter Parker and Miles Morales as you switch between them, utilizing their unique abilities.

  5. Sleeping Dogs

    An open-world action game set in Hong Kong, where you play as an undercover cop infiltrating a notorious Triad organization.
